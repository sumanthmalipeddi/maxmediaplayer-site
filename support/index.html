Subscriptions

MaxMediaPlayer Pro is an auto-renewing subscription with a 1-month free trial. To manage or cancel, open iOS Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → MaxMediaPlayer. Cancelling during the free trial avoids any charge.

Adding videos

Open the Files app → On My iPhone/iPad → MaxMediaPlayer and drag in your video files, or open a file from your own network/cloud storage. MaxMediaPlayer plays only media you already own; it does not download or provide any content.
